THE BOYS


Plot Synopsis:

In a world where superheroes are owned by evil, money hungry, faceless corporations who care nothing but to acquire money, the common people suffer. While the superheroes are idolized as saviors for America, in truth they cause more harm than good and often get innocents killed with little to no consequences. THe protaginist Hughie, has his girlfriend killed due to this universe's version of The Flash and wants any revenge he can get. He meets Billy Butcher, a somewhat psychopathic vigilante who has made it his life's goal to kill every last one of the so called heroes for what one did to his wife.



Hughie

A former electronic store worker that gets his life upturned by his girlfriend getting killed while the superhero responsible gets no consequences aside from giving a not at all sincere apology that was forced out by his corporate bosses

Billy Butcher

A former British Special forces member turned anti supe vigilante. After having his wife be raped and killed by Homelander, the most powerful superhero on the planet, he vowed from that day on he would spend his life destroying Vaught, the company that owns the "heroes" and covers up their crimes

Frenchy

A very French person who is a former colleague whom Billy recruits upon recruiting Hughie in his anti-supe vigilante team.

Mother's Milk

Another former colleague of Butcher that he recruits along the way. However his personal mission for revenge is a tought act to pursue as he must balance that with taking care of his family.

Kimiko

A Japanese girl who was kidnapped and forced to serve in a terrorist organization until being smuggled into the US to be experimented with the formula that gives Superheroes their powers, in a plot to spread them around the world, to justify the US putting supes in the military and justifying their imperialism. She is found the other protaginists and joins their group though it takes much convcincing. She is also the only member of the group with superpowers.

Homelander

The Superman stand-in and by far the most iconic and beloved character of the show, despite being the most evil. He was raised in a lab, created to be the perfect American superhero, and was turned into a narcissistic, fascist, and psychopathic person. Unfortunately, there is little anyone can do, as he is by far the most powerful of the supes in this world. He is Butcher's main rival throughout the show
